## Why ScreenSentinel?

Artificial intelligence is making it easier than ever to create convincing videos.

Most verification tools work **after** a video has already been watched.

ScreenSentinel brings authenticity analysis directly to the moment content is being consumed.

No uploads.

No browser extensions.

No switching tabs.

Just watch.

| Traditional Detection | ScreenSentinel |
|-----------------------|----------------|
| Upload a video | Watch normally |
| Wait for analysis | Instant overlay |
| Open another website | Stay on your screen |
| Verify after watching | Evaluate while watching |
